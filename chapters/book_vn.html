20. chch v Phng sai: Hai ngun ln ca li

Gi s khi hun luyn, tp pht trin v tp kim tra c cng phn phi. Khi bn cn lun c gng thu thp thm d liu hun luyn, v d sao iu cng ch gip ci thin cht lng, ng khng?

Mc d c thm d liu khng gy nh hng xu, th tht khng may, iu ny khng phi lc no cng mang li hiu qu nhiu nh mong i. Vic thu thp thm d liu c th tr nn lng ph thi gian. Vy lm th no quyt nh khi no nn hoc khng nn thm d liu?

C hai ngun li chnh trong hc my: chch v phng sai. Hiu c chng s gip bn quyt nh liu vic thm d liu hay p dng cc k thut khc ci thin cht lng ca m hnh c ng b thi gian ra khng.

Gi s bn hy vng xy dng c mt b nhn dng mo vi 5% li. Hin ti, tp hun luyn v tp pht trin c t l li ln lt l 15% v 16%. Trong trng hp ny, vic thm d liu c th khng gip c g nhiu. Bn nn tp trung vo cc thay i khc. Chc chn rng vic tng s mu cho tp hun luyn ch khin qu trnh hun luyn m hnh trn tp ny tr nn kh khn hn. (L do s c gii thch trong chng sau.)

Nu t l li trn tp hun luyn l 15% (tc chnh xc 85%), nhng mc tiu ca bn l 5% li ( chnh xc 95%), th vn trc tin cn gii quyt l ci thin cht lng thut ton trn tp hun luyn. Cht lng trn tp pht trin/kim tra thng thp hn trn tp hun luyn. Bi vy, nu bn ang c chnh xc 85% trn cc mu m thut ton tng thy, th khng th no t c chnh xc 95% cho cc mu m thut ton cha thy bao gi.

Gi s nh trn rng thut ton ca bn c 16% li ( chnh xc 84%) trn tp pht trin. Chng ta tch 16% li ny ra hai thnh phn:

  • Th nht, t l li ca thut ton trn tp hun luyn, l 15% trong v d ny. Chng ta tm coi gi tr ny nh chch ca thut ton.

  • Th hai, cht lng ca thut ton trn tp pht trin (hoc kim tra) km hn bao nhiu so vi trn tp hun luyn. Trong v d ny, thut ton lm vic km hn 1% trn tp pht trin so vi tp hun luyn. Chng ta tm coi gi tr ny nh phng sai ca thut ton [6].

Mt s thay i trong thut ton hc c th gii quyt thnh phn th nht ca li -- chch -- v ci thin cht lng ca n trn tp hun luyn. Mt s thay i gii quyt thnh phn th hai -- phng sai -- v gip thut ton tng qut ha tt hn t tp hun luyn ti tp pht trin/kim tra [7]. Vic nm c thnh phn no trong hai thnh phn li trn l ng gii quyt hn s rt hu ch trong vic chn ra nhng thay i tim nng nht.

Pht trin trc gic tt v chch v Phng sai s gip bn chn nhng thay i hu hiu cho thut ton.

Phng sai l gim cht lng trn tp kim tra so vi tp hun luyn trong thit lp ny. Khi php o li l sai s ton phng trung bnh, bn c th vit ra cng thc tnh hai i lng ny v chng minh c rng Tng Li = Chch + Phng Sai. Nhng vi mc ch xc nh lm th no to s tin trin trong mt bi ton hc my, th nh ngha t chnh thng hn ca chch v phng sai nh trnh by y l .

21. Nhng v d v chch v Phng sai

Hy xem xt vic phn loi mo ca chng ta. Mt b phn loi "l tng" (nh con ngi) c th t c hiu sut gn nh hon ho cho vic ny.

Gi s thut ton ca bn t c:

  • Li hun luyn = 1%

  • Li pht trin = 11%

Vy vn gp phi l g? p dng cc nh ngha t nhng chng trc, chng ta c tnh chch l 1% v phng sai l 10% (=11%-1%). Do , thut ton c phng sai cao. Tuy b phn loi c li hun luyn rt thp, nhng n li khng khi qut ho c cho tp pht trin. Hin tng ny cng c gi l qu khp.

By gi hy xem xt trng hp sau:

  • Li hun luyn = 15%

  • Li pht trin = 16%

Chng ta c tnh chch l 15% v phng sai l 1%. B phn loi ny khp km vi tp hun luyn vi 15% li nhng li tp pht trin ch cao hn mt cht so vi tp hun luyn. Do , b phn loi ny c chch cao nhng phng sai thp. Chng ta ni thut ton ny ang di khp (underfit).

By gi hy xem xt trng hp sau:

  • Li hun luyn = 15%

  • Li pht trin = 30%

Chng ta c tnh chch l 15% v phng sai l 15%. B phn loi ny c chch cao v phng sai cao: N hot ng km tp hun luyn, do c chch cao, v cht lng ca n trn tp pht trin cn t hn, do n cng c phng sai cao. Thut ng qu khp/di khp rt kh p dng y v b phn loi ng thi b qu khp v di khp.

Cui cng, hy xem xt iu ny:

  • Li hun luyn = 0,5%

  • Li pht trin = 1%

B phn loi ny ang hot ng tt v n c chch thp v phng sai thp. Chc mng bn t c mt hiu sut tuyt vi!

22. So snh vi t l li ti u

Trong v d nhn dng mo ca chng ta, t l li "l tng" -- t l c th t c bi mt b phn loi "ti u" -- l gn vi 0%. Gn nh mi lc, mt ngi nhn vo bc nh c th nhn ra c mo trong hay khng; do chng ta c th hy vng my mc cng lm c iu tng t.

Mt s bi ton khc th kh hn. V d nh chng ta xy dng mt h thng nhn dng ging ni v nhn ra rng 14% cc on m thanh c qu nhiu nhiu nn hoc kh hiu ti mc ngay c con ngi cng khng th nghe c nhng g c ni. Trong trng hp ny, ngay c h thng "ti u" nht cng c th c li khong 14%.

Gi s rng vi bi ton nhn dng ging ni ny, thut ton ca bn t c:

  • Li hun luyn = 15%

  • Li pht trin = 30%

Cht lng trn tp hun luyn hin gn vi t l li ti u l 14%. Do , khng c nhiu ch ci thin chch hoc cht lng trn tp hun luyn. Tuy nhin, thut ton ny khng tng qut ho tt trn tp pht trin; do c rt nhiu ch ci thin li do phng sai.

V d ny tng t nh v d th ba trong chng trc, cng c li hun luyn l 15% v li pht trin l 30%. Nu t l li ti u l xp x 0%, th 15% li hun luyn li nhiu kh nng ci thin. iu ny gi rng nhng thay i lm gim chch c th mang li nhiu kt qu. Nhng nu t l li ti u l 14%, th cht lng tng t trn tp hun luyn cho thy c rt t c hi ci thin chch ca b phn loi.

Vi cc bi ton trong t l li ti u ln hn nhiu mc 0%, th c mt cch phn tch chi tit hn v li ca thut ton. Tip tc vi v d nhn dng ging ni trn, li tng cng trn tp pht trin l 30% c th c chia nh nh sau (phn tch tng t c th p dng cho li trn tp kim tra):

  • T l li ti u (" chch khng trnh c"): 14%. Gi s chng ra quyt nh rng, ngay c khi dng h thng nhn dng ging ni tt nht trn th gii, th ta vn phi chu 14% li. Ta c th coi li l phn "khng trnh c" trong chch ca thut ton hc my.

  • chch trnh c: 1%. Hiu gia li hun luyn v li ti u. [8]

  • Phng sai: 15%. Hiu gia li trn tp pht trin v li trn tp hun luyn.

T nhng nh ngha trc, th mi lin h gia chch v chch trnh c l:[9]

chch = T l li ti u (" chch khng trnh c") + chch trnh c

" chch trnh c" phn nh thut ton ca bn hot ng km hn bao nhiu so vi "b phn loi ti u".

Khi nim phng sai vn gi nguyn nh trc. Theo l thuyt, chng ta lun c th gim phng sai v gn bng khng bng cch hun luyn trn mt tp hun luyn cc ln. Do , tt c phng sai l "trnh c" khi tp d liu ln v khng c ci gi l "phng sai khng trnh c".

Xem xt thm mt v d na, trong t l li ti u l 14%, ta c:

  • Li hun luyn = 15%

  • Li pht trin = 16%

Trong khi chng trc chng ta gi y l mt b phn loi c chch cao, by gi chng ta ni rng li t chch trnh c l 1% v li t phng sai l khong 1%. Do , thut ton ca chng ta l rt tt v c rt t khong trng ci thin. N ch km ng 2% so vi t l li ti u.

T nhng v d ny chng ta thy rng t l li ti u rt hu ch cho vic nh hng cc bc tip theo. Trong thng k, t l li ti u cn c gi l t l li Bayes hay t l Bayes.

Lm sao chng ta bit c t l li ti u? Vi nhng vic m con ngi lm tt, nh nhn dng nh hay phin thoi cc on m thanh, bn c th nh ai gn nhn sau o chnh xc ca nhng nhn ny vi tp hun luyn. iu ny s cung cp mt con s c tnh ca t l li ti u. Nu bn lm vic vi mt bi ton m ngay c con ngi cng kh gii (v d nh d on xem nn gi b phim no, hay hin qung co no trc ngi dng), th c th s kh c tnh c t l li ti u.

Trong phn "So snh vi cht lng mc con ngi (chng 33 ti chng 35)", ti s tho lun chi tit hn qu trnh so snh cht lng mt thut ton hc my vi cht lng mc con ngi.

Trong mt vi chng trc, bn hc cch tnh phng sai v chch trnh c/khng trnh c bng cch xem xt t l li hun luyn v t l li pht trin. Chng tip theo s tho lun v cch bn c th s dng nhng hiu bit su sc t phn tch u tin cc k thut lm gim chch hoc cc k thut lm gim phng sai. C nhiu k thut khc nhau nn p dng tu thuc vo vn hin ti trong d n ca bn l chch (trnh c) cao hay phng sai cao. Hy c tip!

Phn 5: So snh vi cht lng mc con ngi

33. Ti sao cn so snh cht lng mc con ngi?

Nhiu h thng hc my hng ti t ng ha nhng th con ngi lm tt. V d nh nhn dng hnh nh, nhn dng ging ni, v phn loi th rc. Cc thut ton hc cng c ci thin rt nhiu, n mc vt qua cht lng mc con ngi trong ngy cng nhiu tc v.

Hn na, c rt nhiu l do khin vic xy dng mt h thng hc my d dng hn nu bn ang gii quyt mt tc v m con ngi c th lm tt:

  1. D dng thu thp d liu t ngi gn nhn. V d, con ngi nhn din hnh nh mo tt, nn tt nhin vic dng ngi gn nhn s cung cp nhn c chnh xc cao cho thut ton hc.

  2. Phn tch li c th da vo trc gic ca con ngi. Gi s rng mt thut ton nhn dng ging ni hot ng km hn so vi con ngi. Gi d n ghi nhm mt on m thanh thnh "This recipe calls for a pear of apples," (dch l "cng thc nu n ny cn mt qu l ca to") vi li t "pair" tr thnh "pear". Bn c th da vo trc gic v c gng hiu thng tin no m con ngi dng nghe ra c bn ghi thoi chun v dng thng tin ny iu chnh thut ton.

  3. S dng cht lng mc con ngi c tnh t l li ti u cng nh t ra mt "t l li mong mun." Gi s thut ton ca bn tr v 10% li trong mt tc v, nhng con ngi ch li 2%. Da vo , chng ta bit rng t l li ti u bng hoc nh hn 2% v chch c th trnh c mc nh nht l 8%. V vy, bn nn th cc k thut gim chch.

Mc d mc s 3 dng nh khng quan trng, ti thy rng vic t mc tiu v t l li mc hp l s gip y nhanh tin ca nhm. Vic bit thut ton ca bn c chch cao c th trnh c l v cng c gi tr v m ra nhiu ty chn th nghim.

C nhng tc v m ngay c con ngi cng lm khng tt. V d, chn mt cun sch gii thiu cho bn; hoc chn mt tin qung co hin th cho ngi dng trn mt trang web; hoc d on th trng chng khon. My tnh lm vic hiu qu hn hu ht mi ngi trong nhng tc v ny. Vi cc ng dng ny, chng ta gp phi cc vn sau:

  • Kh tin tng trc gic ca con ngi. V d, gn nh khng ai c th d on c th trng chng khon. V th, nu thut ton d on c phiu ca chng ta khng tt hn d on ngu nhin, th tht kh tm ra cch ci thin n.

  • Kh tm ra t l li ti u v t l li mong mun hp l. Gi s bn c mt h thng gii thiu sch ang hot ng kh tt. Lm th no bn bit mnh c th ci thin n thm bao nhiu nu khng c gii php cp con ngi?

34. Cch xc nh cht lng mc con ngi

Gi s bn ang lm vic trn mt ng dng hnh nh y t m t ng a ra chn on t nh X quang. Vi tc v chun on ny, mt ngi bnh thng khng c nn tng y hc no ngoi mt s o to c bn c mc 15% li. Mt bc s tr mi ra trng c mc 10% li. Mt bc s dy dn kinh nghim t c mc 5% li. V mt nhm nh cc bc s cng trao i v tho lun tng nh t c mc 2% li. Vy ci no trong nhng t l li ny c nh ngha l "cht lng mc con ngi"?

Trong trng hp ny, ti s ly 2% lm "cht lng mc con ngi" cho t l li ti u ca chng ta. Bn cng c th t 2% lm mc cht lng mong mun v n tha mn c ba l do (trnh by chng trc) so snh vi cht lng mc con ngi:

  • D dng ly d liu t ngi gn nhn. Bn c th nh mt nhm bc s dn nhn cho bn vi t l li 2%.

  • Phn tch li da vo trc gic. Bng cch tho lun hnh nh vi mt nhm cc bc s, bn c th da vo trc gic ca h phn tch li.

  • Dng cht lng mc con ngi c tnh t l li ti u cng nh t ra "t l li mong mun" kh thi. Vic dng mc 2% lm c lng v t l li ti u l hp l. T l li ti u thm ch c th thp hn 2%, nhng khng th ln hn, v mt nhm bc s c th t c mc 2% li. Ngc li, s khng hp l khi s dng 5% hoc 10% lm c lng cho t l li ti u, v chng ta bit cc mc c tnh ny qu ln.

Khi ly d liu c gn nhn, c th bn khng mun tho lun v mi bc nh vi ton b i ng bc s v thi gian ca h rt ng gi. Nhng bn c th nh mt bc s tr mi ra trng gn nhn cho phn ln cc trng hp v ch dnh nhng trng hp kh hn cho cc bc s c kinh nghim hn hoc cho i ng bc s.

Nu h thng hin ti ca bn c mc 40% li, th vic nh mt bc s mi ra trng (10% li) hay mt bc s c kinh nghim (5% li) gn nhn v a ra nhng phn on trc gic khng em li nhiu khc bit. Nhng nu h thng ca bn ang c 10% li, th vic xc nh cht lng mc con ngi mc 2% s cho bn cc cng c tt hn tip tc ci thin h thng ca mnh.

Ngc li, bn cng c th s dng mt pipeline vi hai bc sau:

53. Phn tch li tng phn

Gi s h thng ca bn c xy dng da trn mt pipeline hc my phc tp v bn mun ci thin cht lng ca n. Bn nn ci thin phn no trong pipeline ny? Bn c th sp xp th t u tin cng vic bng cch quy li c th cho tng phn trong pipeline.

Hy s dng v d b phn loi mo Xim ca chng ta:

Phn u tin, b pht hin mo xc nh v tr ca mo v ct chng ra khi tm nh. Phn th hai, b phn loi ging mo xc nh xem c phi l mt con mo Xim hay khng. Vic ci thin bt k b phn no trong pipeline ny cng c th tn ti hng nm tri. Lm sao bn quyt nh c (nhng) b phn no cn tp trung ci thin?

Bng vic thc hin phn tch li tng phn, bn c th c quy trch nhim cho mt (hoc i khi l c hai) phn trong pipeline trn tng d on sai ca thut ton. V d, thut ton phn loi sai tm nh ny khng c mt con mo Xim trong (y=0) mc d nhn chnh xc l y=1.

Hy kim chng mt cch th cng xem hai bc ca thut ton lm g. Gi s b pht hin mo Xim pht hin ra mt ch mo nh di y:

Tc l b phn loi ging mo c a cho tm hnh sau:

Sau , b phn loi ging mo xc nh chnh xc rng tm hnh ny khng c mo Xim. Vy nn, b phn loi ging mo khng c li: N c a cho cho xem mt ng v tr ra nhn y=0 rt hp l. V trn thc t, con ngi cng phn loi tm nh ton trn l khng c mo m thi -- y=0. Do vy, bn r rng c th quy li ny cho b pht hin mo.

Mt khc, nu gi s b pht hin mo c cho ra kt qu khung cha nh di y:

th bn s kt lun rng b pht hin mo hon thnh cng vic ca n, v li l do b phn loi ging mo m ra.

Gi s bn kim chng 100 tm nh b phn loi nhm trong tp pht trin v nhn ra rng 90 trong s l do li ca b pht hin mo, ch c 10 tm l do li ca b phn loi ging mo. Bn c th an ton kt lun rng mnh nn tp trung nhiu hn vo vic ci thin b pht hin mo.

Ngoi ra, bn cng tm ra 90 mu m b pht hin mo tr v khung cha cha chnh xc. Bn c th s dng 90 mu ny thc hin vic phn tch li k hn trn b pht hin mo v tm cch ci thin n.

Vic lm th no quy li cho mt phn ca pipeline vn ang c m t mt cch khng hp thc: bn nhn vo u ra ca mi phn xem liu c th quyt nh phn no gy ra li. Phng php khng hp thc ny c th l vi bn. Tuy nhin chng sau, bn s thy mt cch hp thc hn ca vic quy li.

54. Quy li cho mt thnh phn

Cng tip tc vi v d ny:

Gi s b pht hin mo cho kt qu khung cha nh sau:

Khi b phn loi ging mo nhn mt vng nh, v cho kt qu khng chnh xc l y=0, tc l khng c con mo no trong hnh.

B pht hin mo hot ng khng tt. Tuy nhin, mt ngi giu kinh nghim vn c th nhn dng mo Xim t bc nh b ct lch. Trng hp ny chng ta nn quy li cho b pht hin mo, b phn loi ging mo, hay l c hai? C s khng r rng y.

Nu s lng cc trng hp khng r rng l nh, th bt k quyt nh no m bn la chn u s t kt qu tng ng. Tuy nhin mt bi kim tra hp thc hn s gip bn quy li chnh xc cho mt thnh phn:

  1. Thay u ra ca b pht hin mo bng mt khung cha th cng:

  1. Np nh b ct tng ng vo b phn loi ging mo. Nu b phn loi ging mo vn phn loi sai th quy li cho b phn loi ging mo. Ngc li th quy li cho b pht hin mo.

Ni cch khc, thc hin th nghim m bn cung cp cho b phn loi ging mo mt u vo "hon ho". Hai trng hp c th xy ra:

  • Trng hp 1: K c vi mt khung cha "hon ho", b phn loi ging mo vn a ra kt qu khng chnh xc y=0. Trong trng hp ny r rng l b phn loi ging mo c li.

  • Trng hp 2: Vi mt khung cha "hon ho", b phn loi ging mo a ra kt qu chnh xc y=1. iu ny cho thy nu b pht hin mo c th a ra khung cha chnh xc hn, th kt qu tng th ca ton h thng s c ci thin. Trong trng hp ny b pht hin mo c li.

Bng cch phn tch cc nh b phn loi sai trn tp pht trin, bn c th quy li chnh xc cho mt thnh phn. iu ny cho php bn c tnh t l li cho tng thnh phn ca pipeline, t quyt nh thnh phn cn tp trung khc phc.

55. Trng hp tng qut ca vic quy li

y l nhng bc tng qut cho vic quy li. Gi s mt pipeline c ba thnh phn A, B v C; trong A cung cp thng tin trc tip cho B, v B cung cp thng tin trc tip cho C.

Vi tng li ca h thng trn tp pht trin:

  1. Th iu chnh th cng kt qu u ra A cho "hon ho" (v d, mt khung cha hnh mo "hon ho"), v sau tin hnh chy thut ton cho pipeline gm c B v C vi u ra ny. Nu thut ton tr v kt qu cui cng chnh xc, iu ch ra rng, thut ton s cho ra kt qu chnh xc nu A tr v kt qu tt hn. Vy ta c th quy li cho A. Nu khng, ta s kim chng thm bc 2.

  2. Th iu chnh th cng kt qu u ra cng on B cho "hon ho". Nu thut ton cho ra kt qu u ra cui cng chnh xc, ta c th quy li cho B. Ngc li, ta tin hnh bc 3.

  3. Quy li cho thnh phn C.

Chng ta hy cng tm hiu mt v d phc tp hn sau y:

Xe t li ca bn s dng pipeline nh trn. Bn s s dng k thut phn tch li tng phn nh th no quyt nh (nhng) thnh phn no cn tp trung ci thin?

Bn c th gi tn ba thnh phn trong h thng l A, B, C tng ng vi cc chc nng nh sau:
A: Pht hin xe hi
B: Pht hin ngi i b
C: Hoch nh ng i cho xe

Vi h thng xe t hnh m t nh trn, gi s bn kim tra xe ca bn trn mt cung ng kn v xc nh trng hp no xe chn hng b li git nhiu hn so vi mt ngi li c kinh nghim iu khin. Trong lnh vc li xe t ng, mt trng hp nh th thng c gi l "tnh hung". Bn cn thc hin:

  1. Th iu chnh th cng kt qu u ra ca thnh phn A (pht hin xe hi) sao cho "hon ho" (v d, cho xe bit v tr ca nhng chic xe khc). Sau tip tc chy phn cn li ca pipeline gm c B, C, nhng cho php C (hoch nh ng i) s dng u ra hon ho ca A. Nu thut ton hoch nh ng i cho xe tt hn, iu cho thy rng, kt qu cui cng ca thut ton t li s c ci thin nu m A tr v kt qu tt hn. Nh vy, bn c th quy li cho A. Nu khng, ta tip tc bc 2.

  2. Th iu chnh th cng kt qu u ra cng on B (pht hin ngi i b) cho "hon ho". Nu thut ton cho kt qu u ra cui cng chnh xc, ta c th quy li cho B. Ngc li, ta tin hnh bc 3.

  3. Quy li cho thnh phn C.

Cc thnh phn ca mt m hnh hc my dng pipeline nn c sp xp theo th c hng khng chu trnh (Directed Acyclic Graph - DAG), ngha l bn c th tnh ton chng theo th t c nh t tri sang phi no , v cc thnh phn sau ch nn ph thuc vo u ra ca cc thnh phn trc . Min l vic xu chui cc thnh phn theo th t A->B->C tun th theo quy tc DAG, th vic phn tch li s din ra tt p. Bn c th nhn c cc kt qu hi khc nhau nu hon chuyn v tr ca A v B cho nhau nh sau:

A: Pht hin ngi i b (trc y l Pht hin xe)

B: Pht hin xe (trc y l Pht hin ngi i b)

C: Hoch nh ng i cho xe

Nhng cc kt qu ca vic phn tch li s vn hp l v cho ta nh hng ti nhng thnh phn no cn tp trung ci thin.
